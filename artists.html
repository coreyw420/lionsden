About Jordy Sam

Jordy Sam is a 20 year old hip hop artist from Bremerton Wa. He's been involved with music since birth with both of his parents being singers. This young man's destiny was set from the jump, and it was known that music was his passion at the age of 2 with him alway trying to play the drums after church on Sundays.

 

At age 7, he became an official musician thru his church. By 9 years old, is when we found he was destined for even more. He started off writing short songs in class in the fourth grade and also around that time, found out about a beat making program called "Fruit Loops". From that time to about his sophomore year he was writing, making beats, recording himself as a hobby in his kitchen, until people started putting in his ear that he could really do something with it.

 

At the age of 18, he put out his first mixtape called "My Experience" with beat productions from himself, and various other producers, but recorded, edited, and mixed and mastered it himself in his bedroom (where he does all of his production). For the next year and a half, he found himself producing 2 other projects for other artist, working on more tracks for himself, and doing shows out in Seattle from time to time.

 

On his 20th birthday, he released his 2nd mixtape called "The Real Good, Feel Good Music Mixtape". Since then, he's been working on more projects, doing shows, and networking with different people around the world.
